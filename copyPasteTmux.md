## LINUX

#### get into copy mode
<kbd>Ctrl</kbd> + <kbd>B</kbd> + <kbd>[</kbd>

<kbd>Space</kbd>
#### highlight whatevs with the keyboard
####
#### then to copy:
<kbd>Enter</kbd>
#### then to paste:
<kbd>Ctrl</kbd> + <kbd>B</kbd> + <kbd>]</kbd>
